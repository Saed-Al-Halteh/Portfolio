  • STAAD.Pro

OpenSTAAD is a library of exposed functions allowing engineers access to STAAD.Pro (Structural Analysis and Design software) Internal functions and commands. In this Program I have been able to reduce modeling time of Steel warehouses significantly.

Assigning cross sections Properties, defining Tapered sections, assigning materials and end releases are performed simply by just entering some parameters in an Excel sheet and then the modeling will be done automatically in STAAD.Pro.

OpenSTAAD has been used to link STAAD.Pro With this Excel sheet allowing for automated modeling and enormously limiting the modeling time.
